Wash your hands with soap and water after touching animals and animal products.

When coughing and sneezing cover your mouth and nose with flexed elbow or tissue.

Avoid travel if you have a fever and cough.

If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing seeking medical care early.

If you are coughing or sneezing, wear a mask and must know how to use it and dispose of it properly.

Eat only well-cooked food.

Avoid close contact with animals that are sick.
